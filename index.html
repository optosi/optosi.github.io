FromSoftware Announces Bloodborne PC Port

The PS4 classic is finally making its way to PC with numerous technical improvements ‐ and it's coming sooner than you might think. Here's everything we know so far.

One of the worst kept secrets in gaming just became very real, very quickly. On December 15, the official FromSoftware Twitter account tweeted that their seminal masterpiece, Bloodborne, has been remastered from the ground up and is coming exclusively to Steam on January 26 of next year.
